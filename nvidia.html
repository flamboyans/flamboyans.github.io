MAX-Q DESIGN

Max-Q design is an innovative approach to designing the world’s thinnest, fastest, and quietest gaming laptops.

For the first time ever, all GeForce GTX GPUs are available in laptops as thin as 18mm. It's all made possible by Max-Q, an innovative approach to designing the world's thinnest, fastest, and quietest gaming laptops. Everything's engineered for the ultimate thin gaming laptop experience, including the laptop, chip, drivers, thermal and electrical components, and more.

Game with next-generation VR performance, the lowest latency, and plug-and-play compatibility with leading headsets—driven by NVIDIA VRWorks™ technologies. VR audio, physics, and haptics let you hear and feel every moment

This groundbreaking display technology delivers the smoothest gaming experience ever. G-SYNC synchronizes the display refresh rates to the GPU in your GeForce GTX-powered laptop, eliminating screen tearing and minimizing display stutter and input lag.

Lose the laptop charger and game on the go. BatteryBoost™ delivers the performance and extended battery life you need to outgun your opponents.

This new, ultra efficient mode makes your plugged-in laptop run much quieter while gaming. It intelligently paces the game’s frame rate while simultaneously configuring the graphics settings for optimal power-efficiency.
