INFINITE SCREEN

CHERRY MX SWITCHES

Feel the difference with each press of the mechanical keys: imbued with Cherry MX switch technology and designed explicitly for gaming with an edge.

LIGHT IT UP

What good would a top-of-the-line keyboard be without a little RGB backlighting? With PredatorSense™, you can tailor the colors to your liking.

MECHANICAL MX BROWN

Mechanical keyboards, heralded by many as essential to gaming, are tactile, responsive and have just the right amount of push without the noise.

FLIP-OVER MODULE

Tactile numpad and precision touchpad in one flippable device with adjustable lighting through PredatorSense™. No matter the situation, you’re ready.

DIAMOND-EDGED

Every detail matters. Emblazoned and cut to diamond-edged perfection, the Predator symbol is stamped upon the cover for all to admire.

SPINNING BRIGHT

The visibly bright AeroBlade™ fan, nestled just outside your reach behind its transparent cover, lights up and spins in a color of your choosing.

DUAL NVIDIA® GTX 1080

While one NVIDIA® GeForce® GTX 1080 is phenomenal, we decided on two – in SLI – because MORE. Each GPU is overclocked and eager for a challenge.

OC THAT CPU

To be the best, you need the best. Unlock real potential with the overclocked 7th Gen Intel® Core i7 processor and put the 21 X to the test.

ENOUGH MEMORY? YEP

5-FAN COOLING SYSTEM

Be one with the cold. Exquisitely designed and deadly efficient, five fans work in unison to maintain a constant cool.

AEROBLADE™

Ultrathin and all metal, three Predator AeroBlade™ fans keep your GPU/CPU at a nice, cool temp. Listen closely as they whisper, “game on, my friend. Game on.”

HEAT PIPES? NINE

That’s right. Nine heat pipes distribute and rid the 21 X of its excess heat. With great power, comes great heat – comes incredible cooling.

GO WITH THE FLOW

With five fans, you better believe the aerodynamic airflow is cold and fast. Before you know it, you might just forget its producing heat at all.

4 SPEAKERS + 2 SUBWOOFERS

Surround yourself with the superior sound of Dolby Audio™ Premium, blaring through a four speaker/two woofer setup. Things are about to get loud.

IT JUST SOUNDS SO GOOD

DOLBY AUDIO™ PREMIUM

TRUEHARMONY™

TrueHarmony™ utilizes a unique design that offers more accurate sound reproduction and fuller-bodied audio.

PREDATORSENSE™

PredatorSense™ is the ultimate tool for control. Take command over lighting, overclocking, game profiles and more. Try not to get lost amidst all the settings.

COOLBOOST™

When the going gets hot, Predator CoolBoost™ gets going. Fan speeds increase based on generated heat, keeping critical areas continually cooled.


DUSTDEFENDER™

Care about your components? Well, so do we. Predator DustDefender™, alongside the 5-fan cooling system, alternates airflow to prevent dust buildup.

PROTECTOR CASE™

Solid. Tough. Really functional. These are just a few of the ways to describe the 21 X’s customized Pelican™ carrying case.

Four polyurethane wheels with stainless steel bearings are pretty much guaranteed to give you the amount of travel time you’ve come to expect from a case of this caliber.

KEEP IT CLEAN

The case itself is watertight, crushproof and dustproof. Even if your 21 X wanted a little grime, it just wouldn’t be possible.

Despite its size, you might occasionally need to travel with the 21 X. Fortunately, there’s convenient placement for everything you need including the machine itself.
